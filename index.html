Frozen Fish (Mackerel, Hake, Croaker)
— Bulk cartoned supply

Premium cartoned frozen fish supplied in consistent, industry-standard sizes for wholesalers, retailers and institutional kitchens. Each shipment is inspected on receipt, packed for optimal cold retention and available in IQF or block-frozen formats to match your handling needs.

Reliable supply cadence and consistent portion yields make menu planning and inventory forecasting simple for buyers at scale.

Frozen Poultry (Chicken & Turkey)
— Whole birds & cut portions

High-quality frozen chicken and turkey lines offered as whole birds or pre-cut portions, packaged and handled under HACCP-aligned procedures. We provide standardized cut sizes, batch traceability and controlled packaging so processors and supermarkets receive predictable yields and easy-to-manage stock.

Contract options and regular delivery schedules reduce procurement friction and stabilise pricing for larger buyers.

Cold-Room Storage & Inventory Management
— Temperature-controlled warehousing

Modern, palletized cold-room facilities with FIFO controls, daily temperature logs and digital inventory snapshots to keep goods fresh and auditable. We offer short- and long-term storage plans, integrated pick-and-pack services and reporting that aligns with procurement cycles and audit requirements.

This reduces spoilage risk, simplifies stock reconciliation and supports seasonal build-ups without capital investment.

Temperature-Controlled Transport & Distribution
— Port-to-market refrigerated logistics

A nationwide refrigerated trucking network that moves product from port or warehouse to market with route optimization, ETA notifications and optional real-time temperature monitoring. Flexible delivery modes include multi-drop routes, dedicated lanes and scheduled contract runs so you can match logistics to demand patterns.

The result is fewer transit losses, improved on-shelf quality and reliable delivery windows for customers across regions.

About Windek Fisheries

Who We Are

Windek Fisheries Limited, registered on March 1, 2016, is a fully incorporated Nigerian company specialising in the wholesale distribution of frozen fish, seafood products, chicken, and turkey. Headquartered in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, we supply retailers, wholesalers and institutions nationwide through reliable cold-chain logistics and long-standing supplier partnerships.

For close to a decade we have built a reputation as a reliable partner by ensuring consistent supply of high-quality, affordable protein across Nigeria. Our operations are strategically positioned to support food security by bridging the gap between global protein supply and domestic demand through transparency, efficiency and customer-centred service delivery.

Our vision is to be the leading and preferred wholesale distributor of quality and affordable fish in Nigeria, and our mission is to distribute affordable fish of the highest quality to retailers while providing exceptional customer service. We operate with core values of Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Partnership and Accountability to ensure product safety, traceability and reliable service for all our partners.
