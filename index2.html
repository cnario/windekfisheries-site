About Windek

Who We Are

Windek Fisheries Limited (RC: 1319250), incorporated March 1, 2016, is a Port Harcourt–headquartered wholesaler and distributor of frozen fish, seafood and poultry serving markets, retailers and institutions across Nigeria.

Vision

To be the leading and preferred wholesale distributor of quality and affordable protein in Nigeria.

Mission

To consistently supply affordable, safe and high-quality frozen fish and poultry to retailers and institutions while maintaining excellent customer service and reliable cold-chain logistics.

Core Values

  • Integrity — Transparent, fair trading
  • Quality — Food safety and product freshness
  • Reliability — On-time deliveries through strong logistics
  • Partnership — Long term relations with suppliers and customers
  • Accountability — Responsible operations and compliance

What We Do

Wholesale supply of frozen fish (mackerel, hake, croaker, horse mackerel, sardines, catfish and others), frozen chicken and turkey, cold-room storage and nationwide distribution using modern cold-chain practices.

Why Choose Windek

  • Established supply relationships and market experience since 2016
  • Dedicated cold-room storage and food-safety practices
  • Flexible ordering (carton, pallet, container) for retailers and institutions
  • Customer-focused service and nationwide reach
